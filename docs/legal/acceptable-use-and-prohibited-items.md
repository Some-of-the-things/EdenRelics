## 1. General standards

1.1 You must not list, sell, buy or ship anything that is unlawful, unsafe, infringing, or prohibited or restricted by this policy or by law.

1.2 You must describe every item honestly and accurately, disclose all flaws, repairs, alterations and odours, and not mislead Buyers about an item's brand, age, authenticity, materials or condition.

1.3 Eden Relics may, at its sole discretion, refuse, remove, edit-request or re-categorise any Listing, and suspend or terminate any Seller, for breach of this policy — see the `seller-agreement.md`.

## 2. Prohibited items — must not be listed

You must **not** list or sell any of the following:

2.1 **Counterfeit, replica or IP-infringing goods.** Fakes, unauthorised copies, or items bearing a brand/logo they are not genuinely from. Only list a branded item if it is genuine. Misusing a brand name to attract searches (e.g. "Chanel style" to sell a non-Chanel item as if it were) is prohibited. *(This is both a legal issue and grounds for immediate removal — see the authenticity warranties in `seller-agreement.md` clause 4.)*

2.2 **Real fur — prohibited on the Marketplace.** You must not list garments or trims made of **real animal fur** (see the note in Section 3 on why vintage sellers must take this seriously). "Faux"/synthetic fur must be genuinely synthetic and labelled as such; if you are not certain an item is faux, do not list it as faux.

2.3 **Ivory and elephant-ivory items.** Items containing **elephant ivory** are severely restricted under the Ivory Act 2018 and generally must not be listed. Certain narrow exemptions exist but require registration/certification; do not rely on them without evidence.

2.4 **CITES / endangered-species materials.** Items made from or containing materials from protected/endangered species — for example **tortoiseshell, certain reptile skins, whalebone/baleen, some feathers/plumage, coral, and other CITES-listed materials** commonly found in vintage accessories — must not be listed unless the sale is genuinely lawful and you hold the required documentation. If in doubt, do not list.

2.5 **Recalled, unsafe or non-compliant goods.** Anything subject to a safety recall, or that does not meet applicable product-safety/labelling requirements (including textile fibre-composition labelling and, where relevant, flammability rules for nightwear).

2.6 **Hazardous materials.** Items containing prohibited hazardous substances (for example asbestos-containing materials), or items that are otherwise dangerous.

2.7 **Weapons and dangerous articles**, controlled drugs, tobacco/vapes, alcohol, live/perishable goods, medicines, and anything else requiring a licence Eden Relics does not support.

2.8 **Stolen goods, or goods you do not own or are not authorised to sell.**

2.9 **Offensive or hateful items**, or items promoting illegal activity or containing illegal content.

2.10 **Non-clothing or off-brand items** outside the Marketplace's curated scope (Eden Relics may restrict the Marketplace to particular categories of vintage womenswear and accessories at its discretion).

> **⚠️ Review note (materials law — high priority):** The fur, ivory (Ivory Act 2018), and CITES/endangered-species rules are **genuinely relevant to vintage clothing and accessories** and are legally complex and changing. Real-fur trade is not currently banned outright in the UK but is heavily regulated and commercially sensitive; ivory and CITES materials carry criminal liability. The solicitor must (a) confirm the exact legal position and current thresholds/exemptions, (b) confirm whether Eden Relics' chosen policy (e.g. banning real fur entirely as a brand choice) is what it wants, and (c) confirm what the Platform must do if a prohibited-materials item is found (removal, reporting obligations). Do not publish Section 2.2–2.6 without this verification.

## 3. Hygiene requirements for second-hand clothing

3.1 All clothing must be **clean and fit to be worn** before it is listed and before it is shipped: laundered/cleaned as appropriate to the garment, and free of stains (unless honestly disclosed), odours (smoke, damp, strong perfume), pet hair and pests.

3.2 Items must be free of **moth/pest infestation**. Do not list or ship anything showing signs of active infestation.

3.3 Certain intimate or hygiene-sensitive items may be restricted or subject to additional rules; see the cancellation exception in `buyer-terms-of-sale.md` clause 7.7 and `returns-and-refunds-policy.md` Section 5. Do not misuse hygiene rules to deny lawful returns.

> **⚠️ Review note:** Confirm the hygiene standard and how it interacts with (i) the CRA "satisfactory quality" duty, and (ii) the narrow hygiene exception to the 14-day cancellation right, so the two are consistent and the exception is not over-applied to ordinary garments.

## 4. Accurate representation

4.1 You must not misrepresent an item's brand, designer, era/date, origin, materials, size/measurements or condition.

4.2 Vintage **dating and attribution must be presented as an honest, informed estimate**, not as guaranteed provenance (see `buyer-terms-of-sale.md` clause 9). Do not state a specific year, maker or provenance as certain unless you have genuine evidence.

4.3 You must not manipulate search, curation, reviews or pricing, or use another party's brand/keywords to mislead.
