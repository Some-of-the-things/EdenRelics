Modern Slavery Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

DCPNET LTD is committed to preventing modern slavery and human trafficking in all aspects of our business and supply chain. This statement is made pursuant to Section 54 of the Modern Slavery Act 2015.

2. Our Business

DCPNET LTD operates as a vintage clothing retailer, sourcing carefully curated garments from a variety of suppliers and sellers across multiple regions.

3. Our Commitment

We are committed to ensuring that there is no modern slavery or human trafficking in our supply chains or any part of our business. We expect the same standards from our suppliers and partners.

4. Due Diligence

We conduct due diligence on all new suppliers and regularly review existing suppliers. We assess and manage the risk of modern slavery occurring in our supply chains.

5. Reporting Concerns

We encourage anyone with concerns about modern slavery in any part of our business or supply chain to contact us at info@dcpnet.co.uk.
