Security Assessment

Last updated: April 2026

1. Our Commitment

Keeping your personal details and payment information safe is our top priority. We regularly test our website and systems to make sure everything is as secure as it should be, and we're always looking for ways to do better.

2. What We Tested

We carried out a thorough security check of our entire platform — including how we handle payments, how we connect to our bank, and how we store and protect your personal information. This involved reviewing the code behind the scenes and testing the site the way a real attacker might try to.

The good news: we found that the site is built on strong foundations. Your connection to our site is always encrypted, your password is stored securely, and our admin areas are properly locked down. We did find some areas we could tighten up, and we've now fixed all of them.

3. Areas We Looked At

  • How card payments are processed and kept secure
  • How our banking connection is protected
  • How your personal data is stored and handled
  • How logins and accounts are protected
  • How we check information you type into the site
  • How our servers and hosting are set up

4. What We Improved

Every issue found during testing has been addressed. Here are the key improvements we made:

  • Your account data can only be accessed by you
  • Login attempts are now limited to prevent automated guessing
  • Signing in with Google, Facebook, or Apple requires proper verification
  • All forms on the site now thoroughly check what's entered before accepting it
  • We've added extra browser-level protections to prevent misuse of the site
  • Error messages no longer reveal any behind-the-scenes details

5. How We Stay Secure

Security isn't a one-off job — it's something we work on continuously. All data sent between your browser and our site is encrypted, passwords are stored using modern hashing so even we can't read them, and we support two-factor authentication for extra protection. We follow industry best practices and carry out regular reviews to stay ahead of new threats.

If you ever spot something that doesn't look right, or have any security concerns, please get in touch at edenrelics@dcp-net.com.
