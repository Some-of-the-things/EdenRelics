Supply Chain Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

DCPNET LTD is committed to responsible and ethical sourcing across our entire supply chain. This policy outlines our standards and expectations for all suppliers and partners.

2. Ethical Sourcing

We source vintage garments through trusted networks of dealers, estate sales, and verified second-hand suppliers. We prioritise transparency and traceability in all sourcing activities.

3. Environmental Responsibility

By specialising in vintage and pre-owned clothing, we actively contribute to reducing textile waste and the environmental impact of the fashion industry. We encourage sustainable practices throughout our supply chain.

4. Supplier Standards

All suppliers are expected to comply with applicable laws and regulations, maintain fair labour practices, and uphold standards consistent with our values of integrity and respect.

5. Monitoring & Review

We regularly review our supply chain practices and supplier relationships to ensure ongoing compliance with this policy. Concerns can be raised at info@dcpnet.co.uk.
