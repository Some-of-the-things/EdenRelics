Why Eden Relics?

Authentically Vintage

Every piece in our collection is a genuine vintage garment, carefully sourced from estate sales, vintage fairs and private collections across the UK and Europe. We never sell reproductions.

Quality Assured

Each dress is inspected, gently cleaned and assessed for condition before it reaches our shop. We grade every item honestly so you know exactly what you are getting, from mint condition to well-loved pieces with character.

Sustainable Fashion

Choosing vintage is one of the most sustainable ways to build your wardrobe. By giving these beautiful garments a second life, you are reducing textile waste and embracing slow fashion without compromising on style.

Spanning the Decades

From flowing 1970s bohemian maxis and bold 1980s power dresses to minimalist 1990s slip dresses, nostalgic Y2K styles and contemporary pieces, our collection celebrates the best fashion from every era.
